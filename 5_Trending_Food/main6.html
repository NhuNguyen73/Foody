    Trending Foods

    Famous dishes in the country of Vietnam

    An Giang

    Banh Cam - Vietnamese Sesame Balls

    Banh Cam are sweet sesame balls made from glutinous rice flour, filled with mung bean paste, and deep-fried. They’re a popular snack or dessert, especially during festivals.

    Sài Gòn

    Banh Mi - Vietnamese Baguette Sandwich with Various Fillings

    Banh Mi is a Vietnamese sandwich with a crispy baguette, filled with meats, pickled vegetables, and fresh herbs. Originating from Saigon, it’s a popular street food.

    Quảng Bình

    Banh Xeo - Savory Vietnamese Pancake with Shrimp and Pork

    Banh Xeo is a crispy pancake made from rice flour, turmeric, and coconut milk, filled with shrimp, pork, and bean sprouts. Popular in central and southern Vietnam.

    Hà Nội

    Bun Cha - Grilled Pork with Vermicelli Noodles and Herbs

    Bun Cha is a Hanoi specialty with grilled pork, vermicelli noodles, and fresh herbs, served with a tangy dipping sauce. It’s a light yet flavorful dish.

    Nha Trang

    Cha Ca La Vong - Grilled Fish with Turmeric and Dill

    Cha Ca La Vong is a Hanoi dish with grilled fish marinated in turmeric and dill, served with vermicelli noodles and fresh herbs. It’s a unique and aromatic dish.

    Sài Gòn

    Com Tam - Broken Rice with Grilled Pork and Fried Egg

    Com Tam is a southern Vietnamese dish with broken rice, grilled pork, and a fried egg, served with pickled vegetables and fish sauce. It’s a hearty meal.

    Southern Vietnam

    Goi Cuon - Fresh Spring Rolls with Shrimp and Pork

    Goi Cuon are fresh spring rolls with shrimp, pork, vermicelli noodles, and vegetables, wrapped in rice paper. Served with a dipping sauce, they’re a healthy appetizer.

    Nam Định

    Pho - Vietnamese Beef or Chicken Noodle Soup

    Pho is a flavorful Vietnamese soup with beef or chicken, rice noodles, and aromatic herbs. Originating from Hanoi, it’s known for its rich broth simmered with spices.
