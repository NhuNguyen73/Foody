    LOCAL FOODS

    The Exquisite Street Food of Vietnam

    Ha Noi

    Local Dishes of Ha Noi

    Am Thuc Ha Noi refers to the rich and diverse culinary scene of Hanoi, known for its unique flavors and traditional dishes. The city boasts iconic foods like Pho (beef noodle soup), Bun Cha (grilled pork with noodles), and Banh Mi (Vietnamese sandwich).

    Sai Gon

    Local Dishes of Ho Chi Minh

    Am Thuc Sai Gon highlights the vibrant and diverse food scene of Ho Chi Minh City. Known for its fusion of flavors, the city offers iconic dishes like, Banh Mi (Vietnamese sandwich), and Com tam (broken rice).

    Hue

    Local Dishes of Hue

    Am Thuc Hue showcases the rich and royal culinary traditions of Hue, known for its intricate flavors and presentation. The city is famous for dishes like Bun Bo Hue (spicy beef noodle soup), Com Hen (rice with baby clams), and Banh Beo (steamed rice cakes).
