    TRADITIONAL FOODS

    The traditional dishes in Vietnam

    Nam Dinh

    Pho Bo – The Rich Flavor of Vietnam

    Pho bo is not only a traditional dish of Vietnam but also a symbol of the country's rich and diverse cuisine. Each bowl of pho bo has a distinctive flavor with a sweet and savory broth, chewy rice noodles, and delicious beef.

    Ha Noi

    Bun Dau Mam Tom Hanoi – A Rustic Delight "Once Tasted, Never Forgotten

    Mentioning Bun dau mam tom, you will find it in every corner of Hanoi, where it has become a popular street food attraction for both domestic and international tourists. This dish's appeal makes it a top suggestion on the list of must-try foods in Hanoi.

    Hue

    Banh Canh Nam Pho – A Unique, Simple Specialty from Dreamy Hue

    Banh canh Nam Pho is a traditional street food that is familiar to culinary enthusiasts in Hue. With its rich, distinctive flavor and simple preparation, it is a must-try regional specialty if you visit Hue.

    Hue

    Banh Xeo – A Flavor That Captivates Diners

    Banh xeo is a rustic, iconic dish of the Vietnamese people. This pancake is famous for its delicious flavor and unique preparation method.

    Hue

    Bun Bo Hue – A Rustic Specialty That Charms Many Diners

    Bun bo Hue is beloved for its eye-catching presentation and unforgettable rich flavor. As a result, Hue attracts visitors not only with the beauty of the Perfume River and Mount Ngu but also with its specialty, bun bo, which is emblematic of the former imperial capital.

    Sai Gon

    Com Tam Saigon – A Beloved Delicacy That Saigonese Take Pride In

    Com tam Saigon is a signature dish of the southern region, considered the traditional and most popular dish in Saigon. With its distinctive flavor, this broken rice dish has become a favorite for many, especially people in the South.

    Sai Gon

    Bap Xao – A Familiar, Nutritious National Street Food

    Bap xao is a street food that is both delicious and budget-friendly. It can be found in many streets and small alleys, offering a unique taste that combines the nuttiness of corn, the richness of scallion oil, and the saltiness of dried shrimp, creating a flavor explosion.

    Sai Gon

    Banh Trang Tron – The Famous Saigon Snack

    Banh trang tron is a popular snack linked to the childhoods of many. The sourness of green mango combined with dried beef, peanuts, chewy rice paper strips, herbs, and sauce creates a taste that captures many hearts.
